About Me

Hello! My name is Andrew Priest, and I am 21 years old. I was born and raised in Portland, Oregon which instilled a strong love of the outdoors, sports, and good food from a young age. I was a very active child, and during my youth I was either playing outside or playing hockey, soccer, or lacrosse (Depending on the season). I went to Catlin Gabel for my middle and highschool years where I made a whole bunch of lifelong friends and learned from passtionate and inspiring teachers. Eventually, it became time to "leave the nest" and go off to college. After an interesting couple of pandemic years, I had decided that I should go try living on the east coast, a decision that would land me at Babson college.

I also chose Babson because of the colleges diversity of education. I knew I wanted to do something in the world of business, but had no clue what that would be. Babson provided me the ability to "test the waters" on a bunch of different sectors. Before I had gone to Babson I believed I wanted to be in econ or finance, but after my first semester I knew that was not the path for me. Additionally, coming from a small highschool, the lack of large lectures and close relationship with professors was something that really stood out to me. It was a tough transition, knowing nobody before I came out here, but I quickly made some really good friends and eventually found a subject that I had some aptitude and enjoyment in. I really enjoy stats and coding so I naturally fell into business analytics and while I have no job lined up at this point, that is what I hope to continue doing after I graduate.

My Interests
